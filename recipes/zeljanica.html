Zeljanica

Description

Zeljanica is a traditional Bosnian savory pie made with phyllo dough and a filling of greens such as spinach or Swiss chard. It is typically served as a main dish or side dish.

Sastojci :

  • 1 lb phyllo dough
  • 1 lb fresh spinach or Swiss chard, washed and chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted

Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the chopped greens, salt, and pepper. Cook until the greens are wilted, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool. In a bowl, mix together the beaten eggs, sour cream, and milk. Add the cooled greens mixture and stir to combine. Lay out the phyllo dough and brush each sheet with melted butter. Place the greens filling along one edge of the dough and roll the dough into a log, tucking in the sides as you go. Place the zeljanica in a greased baking dish and brush the top with water. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until golden brown. Serve hot.
